## Description

*Artemis* is Andy Weir's second novel, following the massive success of *The Martian*. Set in the near future, the story takes place in Artemis — humanity's first and only city on the Moon. The protagonist is Jazz Bashara, a young woman who works as a porter but moonlights (pun intended) as a smuggler. When a seemingly simple crime-for-hire goes spectacularly wrong, Jazz finds herself in the middle of a dangerous conspiracy that threatens the entire city.

Like *The Martian*, Weir grounds the story in real science and engineering: the economics of a lunar colony, the physics of low gravity, the chemistry of life support systems. Jazz is a witty, irreverent narrator — flawed, funny, and surprisingly relatable for a character living 384,000 km from Earth.

> "I'm not stupid. I'm just not motivated to be smart very often."
